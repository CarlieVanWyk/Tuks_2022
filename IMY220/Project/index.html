What do we do?

See You Soon is the home of all your favourite events! Here you can create events, make them available to friends and family, and see what events other people are attending. You can also see what events you are attending. See You Soon helps you organize your events and share them with others. You are free to create any type of event, be it weddings, music festivals, or food markets.

What we can offer you:

  • Creating events
  • Sharing events
  • Attending events
  • Deleting events
  • Viewing events
